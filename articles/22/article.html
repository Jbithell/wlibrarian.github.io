Library News, Issue 30

Agreement has been reached over editorial control of The Librarian, confirming editorial independence and a new format of The Librarian.

The Librarian has also launched a website, available at https://librarian.cf.

A new one page summary of The Librarian will be available from the water cooler within the next week.

The Librarian is as usual open to submissions of articles. Isky Mathews and Ben Randall Shaw have started a new series Adventures in Recreational Mathematics; Isky has kindly submitted the first article ready for this edition.1
